# MiroFish

**MiroFish** is a next-generation AI prediction engine powered by multi-agent technology. By extracting seed information from the real world, it constructs a high-fidelity parallel digital world inhabited by thousands of intelligent agents to simulate and predict future trajectories like breaking news, policy drafts, and financial signals.

## Core Mechanisms
- **Macro Level:** A rehearsal lab for decision-makers (policy, public relations tests).
- **Micro Level:** A creative sandbox for users (novel endings, creative simulations).
- **Workflow:** Graph Building $\rightarrow$ Environment Setup $\rightarrow$ Simulation $\rightarrow$ Report Generation $\rightarrow$ Deep Interaction.
